About Me

What drives me?

Ever since I was a child, I’ve been fascinated by computers. That early curiosity to understand how humans communicate with machines eventually led me to discover the world of programming. Today, what drives me is not just building interactions, but creating meaningful experiences. I'm especially drawn to projects that save people time—because I believe time is the most valuable resource we can give back.

Education

I'm currently studying front-end development through hands-on practice, projects, and online platforms like Codecademy. I focus on mastering HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I’m always exploring how to build clean, efficient, and responsive interfaces.

Experience
